Team Mini Competitions - Web

You are tasked with building an online flight booking website. (eg. Expedia) A portal to manage airlines/flights, and a front-facing portal for customers to book tickets.

November Registration: Google Form

Requirement(s)

  • Customers should be able to see a list of flights from airport A to airport B.
  • Customers should be able to see a list of flights for a selected date.
  • Customers should be able to filter a list of flights by at least 1 other criteria. (eg. airlines, price range)
  • Customers should be able to sort a list of flights by at least 1 criteria in both ascending and descending order. (eg. travel time, price)
  • Customers should be able to book a flight with available seats.
  • Customers should be able to cancel their booked flight at most 24hrs before departure.
  • Customers should be able to see their flight ticket information.
  • Customers should be able to leave a review for a previous flight they’ve went on.
  • (Optional) Customers should be notified by email of a change in flight information (eg. postponed, canceled).
  • Airlines should be able to add new flights.
  • Airlines should be able to postpone their registered flights.
  • Airlines should be able to cancel their registered flights.
  • Users should be able to register a Customer account.
  • Users should be able to register an Airline account on behalf of an airline.

Notes

  • The application must NOT request NOR collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII)* except name, email address.
  • For any questions about what other information may constitute PII, consult Gideon or Nikolas.
  • Extra points for unlisted novel requirements are assigned subject to the discretion of the judge(s).
PII includes, but is not limited to: name, email address, date of birth, credit card information, student number, address, phone number, passport information, driver’s license information etc.
