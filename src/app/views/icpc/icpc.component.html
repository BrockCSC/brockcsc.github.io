Team Mini Competitions

You are tasked with building an online investment platform e.g., QuestTrade A portal to manage investments manually or via investment broker / AI.
You will be using a fake stock market for investments, create and hosted explicitly for this contest.
Do not abuse the API in any way.

January Registration: Google Form

Requirement(s)

  • Users should be able to register an account
  • Customers should be able to review stock information
  • Customers should be able to review portfolio information and notifications about owned stocks and markets
  • Customers should be able to add and remove money from their portfolio
  • Customers should be able to choose between a self-managed account (manual investing) and an automated account (transactions done by AI/someone/something else) when creating their portfolio
  • Customers should be able to purchase and sell stock shares from the funds in their portfolio, during active market trading hours

Notes

  • The application must NOT request NOR collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII)* except name, email address.
  • For any questions about what other information may constitute PII, consult Nikolas.
  • Extra points for unlisted novel requirements are assigned subject to the discretion of the judge(s).
PII includes, but is not limited to name, email address, date of birth, credit card information, student number, address, phone number, passport information, driver's license information etc.
